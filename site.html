Frequently asked questions

Is Karvo free for customers?

Yes — browsing, searching, and booking a pro costs you nothing. You only pay the pro directly for the work, at the rate you agreed before the job started.

How are pros verified?

Every pro submits their ID and details when registering. Our team verifies them before they're listed, and customer ratings after every job keep the quality honest.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

Message us on WhatsApp within 24 hours. We'll follow up with the pro, and repeated complaints get a pro removed from Karvo. Your rating also warns other customers.

Do I pay online or in cash?

You pay the pro directly when the job is done — cash or mobile wallet, whatever suits you both. Karvo never asks for advance payment.

I'm a skilled worker. How do I join?

Tap "Register as a pro", fill in your details, and send them to us on WhatsApp. Once verified, you'll start receiving job requests from your area — joining is free.

Trust & safety

Your safety comes first

Inviting someone into your home is a matter of trust. Here's exactly how Karvo works to keep every job safe for customers and pros alike.

How we verify pros

  • Every applicant submits their CNIC and contact details before listing.
  • We call and interview each pro about their trade and experience.
  • New pros start with a "New on Karvo" period while they build ratings.
  • Repeated complaints or a rating below 4.0 leads to removal.

What to expect on job day

  • Your pro confirms the agreed price before starting — never pay more without agreeing first.
  • Pay only when the work is finished, in cash or wallet, directly to the pro.
  • Never pay any advance to anyone claiming to be "from Karvo" — we never ask for advance payment.

Safety tips

  • Keep the booking details in WhatsApp — it's your record of the agreed job and price.
  • If possible, have a family member home during the visit.
  • Rate every job honestly — your rating protects the next customer.
  • Anything feels wrong? Message us immediately on WhatsApp and we'll step in.
